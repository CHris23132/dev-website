Privacy Policy

Bumper Bids respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have as a driver.

Trip Data. We collect trip data only to the extent of total distance traveled. We do not track exact paths, turn-by-turn details, or detailed trip routes.

General Location. We use general location information solely to determine which ads are available in your area. This keeps the experience relevant without tracking precise trip details.

Data Sharing. All information shared is held confidential and secure. We do not share any driver data with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

Security. We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data.

Retention. We retain information only for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Choices. You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your data, subject to applicable law.
