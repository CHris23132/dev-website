Privacy Policy

Data Collection Policy

At Anti Scam AI, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. Our primary goal is to help you travel safely while maintaining the highest standards of data protection.

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from our users. Our application operates entirely on your device without transmitting any personal information to our servers.

Location Permission Usage

Our app requests location permissions for the following specific purposes:

  • Local Rate Analysis: To provide accurate suggestions for local rates and pricing in your current area
  • Red Flag Detection: To identify potential scams and bad offers based on comparison with local market data
  • Geographic Context: To ensure recommendations are relevant to your specific location

How Location Data is Used

When you grant location permission, our app:

  • Uses your location to access local market data and pricing information
  • Compares offers you receive against typical rates in your area
  • Identifies potential red flags based on local pricing patterns
  • Does not store, track, or transmit your location data
  • Processes all location-based analysis locally on your device

Local Processing Only

All analysis, translation, and scam detection happens locally on your device. We do not have access to any of the offers you analyze, your location history, or any information about your usage of the app.

No Data Storage

As a company, we do not collect, store, or maintain any data about our users. Your privacy is our top priority, and we have designed our application to respect this commitment while still providing valuable protection against scams.

Third-Party Services

Our app may access publicly available market data and pricing information to provide accurate comparisons. This data is accessed anonymously and does not involve any personal information.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about our privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at play3dinc@gmail.com.
