Privacy Policy

At Real Strike Warfare, we take your privacy seriously. We want to be completely transparent about our data practices:

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data. Our application uses your device's camera solely for the purpose of powering our computer vision system, which:

  • Does not record or capture any images or video
  • Does not build user profiles
  • Does not collect or store any user data
  • Only uses real-time processing for object detection and human figure detection to enable gameplay

The computer vision system operates in real-time on your device, processing the camera feed instantly without storing or transmitting any data. This technology is used exclusively to create an interactive gaming experience, similar to how a motion sensor works in traditional gaming consoles.

We believe in creating a fun, immersive experience while respecting your privacy. Our technology is designed to be secure, local, and completely private.
