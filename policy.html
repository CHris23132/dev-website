Privacy Policy

Data Collection Policy

At Time Travel AI, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information.

No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data from our users. Our application operates entirely on your device without transmitting any information to our servers.

Permissions Usage

Our app requests the following permissions strictly for creating personalized stories:

  • Location Permission: Used only to create stories that take place in your current setting. We do not store, track, or transmit your location data.
  • Camera Permission: Used only to capture images for story creation. All images are processed locally on your device and are never saved to our servers.

Local Processing Only

All story generation and image processing happens locally on your device. We do not have access to any of the content you create or any information about your usage of the app.

No Data Storage

As a company, we do not collect, store, or maintain any data about our users. Your privacy is our top priority, and we have designed our application to respect this commitment.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at play3dinc@gmail.com.
