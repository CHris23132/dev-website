Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 22 2025

Last Updated: April 22 2025

1. What Data We Collect

We collect the following data when you use our app:

  • Name, age, gender, and profile information
  • Real-time camera photos (required for profile creation)
  • Location (to suggest nearby safe meeting places)
  • Device and diagnostic data (for performance and security)
  • Behavior and usage (e.g., swipes, likes, matches)
  • Profile analysis data (used for matching/classification)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use this data to:

  • Display your profile to other users
  • Suggest nearby users and meeting places
  • Maintain app functionality and performance
  • Help classify profiles for better matches
  • Improve safety by ensuring real-time photo uploads

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for their direct marketing.

3. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored on our servers. This includes:

  • Your profile
  • Your image(s)
  • Match preferences and interaction history
  • Classification information for your profile

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and data anytime.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not currently use ad networks or third-party tracking tools. However, in the future, we may integrate services such as:

  • Google AdMob
  • Facebook Audience Network

If we do, those services may collect identifiers and track your usage to serve personalized ads. You will be notified and asked for permission where required.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your country or province/state:

  • You may access, update, or delete your data
  • You can withdraw consent at any time
  • You may contact us for data inquiries: play3dinc@gmail.com

6. Children

Our app is not for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover this, the account will be deleted.

7. Security

We use encryption and secure access controls to protect your data. However, no method is 100% secure — by using the app, you accept this risk.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. You'll be notified of material changes.
