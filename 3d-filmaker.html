In-Camera VFX Made Simple

3D Filmmaker is an augmented reality filmmaking app that lets you create scenes with tracked 3D objects, virtual backgrounds, and animated characters—right inside your real environment. No complicated setup and no physical green screen required.

Place and anchor elements to walls, floors, and real-world surfaces, then film from different angles while everything stays locked in place. Set your shot, direct your actors, and capture the scene with video and audio recorded in-app.

In-Camera VFX

Add 3D objects into your real space using AR placement. Anchor props to surfaces so they stay tracked and stable. Use virtual backgrounds for “green screen” style shots without a green screen.

Build Full Scenes

Drag, rotate, scale, and position objects with intuitive controls. Add animated characters with built-in actions. Preview your scene in real time while you compose your shot.

Cinematic Look

Apply LUTs (live color grading) over the camera view. Adjust intensity and opacity to match your style before you record.

Private by Design

We don’t save any data. The app runs 100% locally on your device—no data collection whatsoever.
